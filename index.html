Who We Are

A leading independent Turkish law firm

Lawrence&Partners is an Istanbul-based independent Turkish law firm with over 130 employees, offering legal advice and counselling to foreign investors and the Turkish business community.

Founded in 1997 by Serdar Paksoy, the firm was established with a commitment to providing result-oriented, creative, innovative, fast and high quality legal advice at international standards.
