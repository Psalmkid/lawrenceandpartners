Who We Are

An Istanbul-based independent Turkish law firm

Lawrence&Partners is an Istanbul-based independent Turkish law firm with over 130 employees, offering legal advice and counselling to foreign investors and the Turkish business community. We provide a wide range of services to meet the needs of local and international businesses in almost every field, including corporate law, capital markets, mergers and acquisitions, competition law, banking and finance, tax, real estate and project development, project finance, energy and infrastructure, litigation and arbitration.

Founded in 1997 by Serdar Lawrence, the firm was established with a commitment to providing result-oriented, creative, innovative, fast and high quality legal advice to the local and international businesses at international standards.
